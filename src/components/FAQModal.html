Frequently Asked Questions

General:

Where can I play the latest version?
Desktop Only so far.
or download the Windows client:
https://github.com/RedSparr0w/Pokeclicker-desktop/releases/latest

Where is the wiki?
https://pokeclicker.miraheze.org/

My game isn't running at full speed when the window isn't active.
This is a setting on browsers that slows down inactive windows,
You can change the browser flags, but this is not recommended,
Otherwise you can download the Windows client from here:
https://github.com/RedSparr0w/Pokeclicker-desktop/releases/latest

How do I get Scan Coins?
Read the in game Tutorial
Set the "Scanned" selector in the top left to Digiscan to scan Digimon you have already scenned.

How do I get Stat Coins?
Every Digimon has a chance to drop one when it is scanned. The type of coin dropped depends on the Digimon scanned.

Saves:

Can I import my save from the previous version?
You can only import the shinies you obtained in the previous version.
They will only appear in your Digimon list once re-scanned
(this will not count as capturing a new dot Digimon)

Will my progress be carried over on release?
Your save file will likely be compatible with the final release.

Rare/Dot Digimon:

What are the odds of finding a roaming Digimon?
The chance to encounter a roaming Digimon is1/8192 for the first route of a region, and increases linearly up to 1/4096 for the last route of a region.
"first" and "last" here just being the route difficulty/HP, not necessarily the order you reach the route

What are the odds of finding a dot Digimon?
On a Route 1/8192
In a Dungeon 1/4096
In the Safari Zone 1/2048
Using an Evolution Item 1/2048
From a Shop 1/2048
From Hatching an Egg 1/1024
Wandering in the Shop 1/1024

Do dot Digimon give any bonus to gameplay?
Yes, they give an extra 50% bonus to Click Attack compared to the non dot variant.

If I breed a shiny Pokémon will it still stay shiny?
Yes, once a Digimon is dot, it will always remain dot.

If I evolve a dot Digimon will the evolution be dot?
No, you will need to obtain the evolutions dot via a different method.

Items:

What is the small/medium/large restore used for?
They can be used in the junkyard to restore some stamina.

What are app disks used for?
These items are currently only used in the Disk Shop to make more disks and earn money.
Note: They will eventually have more uses.

How many Hina Item slots are available?
There is a maximum of 3 active alots.

Gameplay:

How many regions are playable?
Currently only Adventure01 is able to be played.

How do I get to the next region?
Note: Once you go to the next region you will not be able to go back to previous regions until you reach the dock.

How do I increase my Click Attack?
New Digimon you scan increase your click attack, dots also increase your click attack at 50% efficiency.

Can I progress towards more than 1 quest at a time?
Yes, you can unlock up to 4 quest slots.
Quest Level 10: 2 slots.
Quest Level 15: 3 slots.
Quest Level 20: 4 slots.

Where is x location?
Junkyard: Route 11.
Daycare: Route 5, or click List on the hatchery.
Disk Shop: Route 14.

How do I unlock x location?
Underground: Buy the Explorer Kit from Whamon's Gulf.
Daycare: Will be unlocked after reaching route 5.
Farm: Will be unlocked after reaching route 14 and obtaining Disks.

Why should I be breeding Digimon?
Some Digimon can only be obtained by breeding.
When a Digimon is hatched, it's attack will increase by 25% of the base amount.
Breeding has higher odds of obtaining a dot Digimon (1/1024).

How do I obtain x Digimon?
Check you have completed every route.
Check each dungeon has been completed.
Some Digimon only come from eggs.
Some can only be obtained by using an Evolution Item.
Some can only be found in the Safari Zone (7 exclusives).
Fossil Pokémon can only be obtained with the correlating Fossil from the Underground.
Some can only be purchased from a Shop.
(Roamers)Some will randomly appear on routes.
(Babies) Breed their evolution.
Some can only evolve at certain times of the day.
Some can only evolve in specific locations.
Some can only appear/evolve in specific weather.
